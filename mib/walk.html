The data shown in this portion of the MIB is populated from cvadmin by parsing the 'show long' command for a particular Volume with additional affinity-specific data being taken from parsing 'cvlabel'. The size and utilization figures are calculated as totals of all the Stripe Groups that are in the Affinity.

The objects in this portion of the MIB are indexed by the xsanVolumeIndex to which the Affinity belongs. For example, xsanAffinityName.1.2 is the second Affinity in the Xsan Volume at xsanVolumeName.1 in the MIB.
